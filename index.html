Features

Create Classes

Create and manage classes for your students.

This feature allows teachers or educators to easily create virtual classes or courses for their students. They can organize their students into different classes based on subjects, grade levels. Within each class, they can manage various aspects such as adding students, scheduling class sessions, and tracking student progress

Video Calls

Engage with your students through interactive video calls.

The video calls feature enables real-time communication between teachers and students through interactive video sessions. These video calls can support features like screen sharing, virtual whiteboards, chat functionalities, enhancing the overall learning experience. No need for physical presence.

Assignments

Effortlessly assign and grade assignments online.

With this feature, teachers can create, distribute, and manage assignments digitally. They can effortlessly assign tasks, projects, quizzes, or assessments to individual students or entire classes. This streamlines the assignment workflow, reduces paperwork, set deadlines, and monitor student progress in real-time.
